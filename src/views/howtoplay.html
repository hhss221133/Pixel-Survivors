How To Play

Pixel Survivors is a 2D pixel game where players compete to defeat the boss as quickly as possible. You have 4 minutes to defeat the boss and can choose to play as the Knight or the Wizard.

Cheating is available by pressing the backspace key (toggle). You will walk faster, have 10x attack power and 10x score while cheat is toggled on.

Characters

Knight

Movement: W A S D
Attack: K
Cheat-key: backspace (toggle)
Features:
  • Short range
  • Walk faster
  • Attack cooldown is lower
  • Can attack more than one enemy at once

Wizard

Movement: W A S D
Attack: Left Mouse Click
Cheat-key: backspace (toggle)
Features:
  • Long range
  • Walk slower
  • Attack cooldown is higher
  • Can only attack one enemy at once (the magic it spawns can only hit one enemy)
